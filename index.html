THE FORMAT

How It Works

Three weeks of sim racing. Post the fastest lap on the SIM1Racing server each week. Top the standings when it's all over and you're heading to the real track — for free.

01
Join the Competition
Log on to the SIM1Racing sim-racing server and set a lap on the week's assigned circuit. Any clean lap counts — as many attempts as you like within the week.
02
Three Weeks, Three Tracks
A new track is set each week. Your fastest lap for the week is your score. Miss a week? Still worth competing — a strong result in weeks 2 or 3 can move you up the standings.
03
Top Two Win
When the final week closes, 1st place takes the two-day weekend pass ($900 value) and 2nd place wins a single-day ticket ($500 value) to Lime Rock Park June 19–20.
📌 Important — Event Date

The track day is June 19–20, 2026 at Lime Rock Park, CT. Winners must be able to attend. The prize is a ticket — all other costs (travel, lodging, optional car rental) are the winner's responsibility. Prizes are non-transferable and have no cash value.
