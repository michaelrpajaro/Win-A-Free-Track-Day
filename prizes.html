Your Roadmap

How to Win

Most prizes need nothing more than showing up. Here's the quick rundown.

01
Pick a Prize
Browse the cards above and choose a prize (or all of them). Note whether it has a sign-up deadline or is open-entry.
02
Register or Enter
Click the card's button to enter — some link to Acuity Scheduling, others to Instagram or a simple sign-up form.
03
Race & Compete
For league prizes, show up on Monday nights and put in fast laps. Gold Members race free; non-members pay per season.
04
Claim Your Win
Winners are contacted by email within 48 hours of the draw or season finale. Prize payouts and shipping arranged directly.
